What's covered?

accident

Based on the details of the car entered by you, our algorithm analyses and suggests a highly competitive price of your car.

Fire

Seeing your car catch fire can be horrifying. We cover damages on the car caused by self-ignition, lightning and explosion.

theft

Finding your car stolen is a nightmare come true. But we’ll pay you your car’s insured value if your car ever does get stolen

Third party losses

Car accidents don’t stop at a single vehicle. Injuries or death to a third party or property damage can occur in certain cases. Damages related to third-party losses are covered by us.

Car owner cover

The car may not be the only casualty. Our cover also extends to injuries sustained by you, in case you sustain any.


What's Not covered?

Illegal Driving

In cases of driving without having a valid driving license, or under influence of alcoholic drinks and/or other illegal substances, you are not covered by us.

Non-Accidental Damage

Breakdowns, wear & tear and deductibles aren’t covered by us. Similarly, consequential damages or liabilities that arose due to car accidents are unfortunately not covered by us.

Tyres,Tubes & Engines

Non-accidental damage done to the tyres, tubes and engine is not covered.
